## 1. Scope of practice — what QHHT is and isn't

QHHT is a guided hypnosis-based modality developed by the late Dolores Cannon. It is:

- **Not** a medical treatment, diagnosis, or cure.
- **Not** psychotherapy, counseling, or psychiatric care.
- **Not** licensed in the way that medicine, nursing, or psychology are licensed in Florida.
- **A complementary, alternative practice** that supports self-discovery and personal insight.

## 3. Claims the site CAN make

- QHHT is a hypnosis-based modality developed by Dolores Cannon.
- Sessions are 5 hours in a relaxed, guided setting.
- Clients often report clarity, insight, and a sense of integration after sessions.
- The session is recorded; clients receive an audio recording.
- A follow-up call within 30 days is included.

## 4. Claims the site CANNOT make

- "Cures," "treats," "heals," or "alleviates" any disease or condition.
- "Diagnoses" anything.
- Replaces medical, psychological, psychiatric, or pharmaceutical care.
- Specific medical or psychological outcomes attributable to the session.
- Spiritual outcomes that imply guaranteed religious/spiritual experience.
