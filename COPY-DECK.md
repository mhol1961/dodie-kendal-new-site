### Chapter 01 — The Early Years: Ritual and Questioning

I am a native Floridian, raised in the tradition of the Catholic Church. From baptism and CCD to Catholic High School, the structure of religion was always around me. Yet, inside, I felt a disconnect. The ceremonies often felt intimidating rather than comforting, and as a young woman, I struggled to find the spiritual side of the actions I was performing. By my teens, I felt that church was simply for "old people," and I stepped away to find my own way.

---

### Chapter 02 — The Search for Something More

While teaching aerobics and navigating the typical unwise choices of youth, I met a young woman whose vibrant faith challenged my perceptions. She didn't fit my "old person" mold of religion. Following her lead, I found myself in a non-denominational Pentecostal church, witnessing things that both perplexed and intrigued me — tongues, dancing, and a raw, supernatural energy I hadn't seen before.

When asked the pivotal question — *"Dodie, do you know where you will go if you died tonight?"* — I realized I didn't have an answer. Motivated by a mix of curiosity and the fear of eternal damnation, I invited Jesus into my heart. It was a time of high energy and "Whooo hooo!" moments, but it also brought my first real lesson in the complexity of healing.

I chose to abandon that belief system for a new perspective: **God is Energy.** A loving, universal energy that encompasses all humans. In this space, only love exists, and free will is the greatest gift we have been given.

### Hero

### What is QHHT — deep section

Think of your mind like a vast library. Your conscious mind is the front desk — busy, logical, handling the daily tasks. But behind those doors is the Subconscious, a deep archive that holds the records of everything your soul has ever experienced.

QHHT is a gentle, guided way into that archive. We use the Theta brainwave state — the threshold between waking and dreaming — so the conscious noise quiets and the Subconscious can speak clearly. You stay in the driver's seat the whole time. You set the questions. Your Higher Self answers.

This is not regression hypnosis. It's not therapy. It's not channeling. It's not a performance. It is what Dolores Cannon called a *Sacred Technology* — and that's a fair description.

---

### What to expect (4-step timeline)

**01 · Pre-session call (30 min)**
We talk on the phone or over Zoom. You bring the questions you want your Higher Self to answer. There's no wrong question. I'll help you shape them.

**02 · Session day (5 hours)**
You arrive at the studio (or settle in for a remote session). We share a conversation, then a short guided induction into the Theta state. The session itself usually runs 3–4 hours; you'll feel like you woke from a deep nap.

**03 · The recording**
Most of what's said you won't remember in detail — the Theta state is like that. I record everything (audio only, with your consent). You take the recording home.

**04 · Integration**
The week after a session often unfolds quietly. Old patterns sometimes loosen. New clarity surfaces. I'm available for one follow-up call within 30 days at no extra cost.
