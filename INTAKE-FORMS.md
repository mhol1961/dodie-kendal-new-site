## 1. Purpose

The intake form serves three purposes:

1. **Inform** the client about what QHHT is and isn't (scope of practice).
2. **Document** voluntary consent to participate in a hypnosis-based session.
3. **Capture** preparation information that helps Dodie tailor the session.

## 2. Delivery mechanism (Phase 1)

- PDF delivered after booking confirmation (via GHL workflow).
- Client fills, signs (wet signature or PDF e-signature), and returns to Dodie before session day.

## 3. Delivery mechanism (Phase 2 — optional)

- Digital intake form on the site (`/intake` route, gated by booking confirmation token).
- DocuSign-style e-signature integration.

## 4. Form contents

### Section A — Identifying information

- Full legal name
- Preferred name
- Date of birth
- Phone
- Email
- Emergency contact: name + phone + relationship
- City, state of residence

### Section B — Health screening

- Are you currently being treated for any of the following: epilepsy or seizure disorder, schizophrenia, dissociative identity disorder, bipolar disorder in an active manic phase, or psychosis? (yes / no) *— if yes, Dodie may decline or request medical clearance before proceeding.*
- Are you currently taking medication that affects consciousness (e.g., anti-psychotics, heavy sedatives)? (yes / no)
- Have you used any psychoactive substances in the past 48 hours? (yes / no)
- Do you have any physical conditions Dodie should know about for comfort during a 5-hour session? (text)

### Section C — Intentions

- Three questions you'd like your Higher Self to address.
- Anything else you want Dodie to know before the session.

> Signature: _______________________   Date: _____________

### Section E — Recording consent (optional secondary)

- ☐ I consent to Dodie reviewing the session recording for her own practice notes.
- ☐ I do NOT consent to the recording being reviewed by anyone but me.

### Section F — Cancellation acknowledgment

- I understand that the $50 deposit is non-refundable if I cancel within 48 hours of the session start time.
- I understand that I may reschedule once at no additional cost if I notify Dodie at least 48 hours in advance.
